# Salary Slip / Pay Slip

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Salary Slip
* **Alternative Names**: Pay Slip, Payslip, Salary Certificate, Earning Statement
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Income, Proof of Employment, Loan eligibility
* **Issuing Authority**: Employer / Company HR or Finance Department

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Employee Name**: Full name of the employee.
* **Employee ID**: Code assigned by employer.
* **Employer Name / Company Name**: Name of the issuing employer.
* **Designation / Job Title**: Employee's position.
* **Month and Year**: The pay period (e.g., "Salary Slip for May 2026").
* **Gross Salary / Net Salary / Take-Home Pay**: Numeric salary values.
* **Deductions**: PF (Provident Fund), Professional Tax (PT), Income Tax (TDS).
* **EPF Account Number / UAN**: Universal Account Number (UAN) for retirement savings in India.
