# Signature Specimen

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Signature Specimen
* **Alternative Names**: Signature Upload, Digital Signature, Sign image, Specimen Signature
* **Primary Uses**: Verification of Consent, Application signing
* **Issuing Authority**: Self-signed on plain white paper and scanned/photographed

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Visual Signature**: Handwritten representation of the applicant's name or custom signature style, typically on white background.
