# Academic Transcript

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Academic Transcript
* **Alternative Names**: Marksheet, Grade Card, Consolidated Transcript, Degree Certificate Transcripts
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Academic Qualification, Course Grade Verification, GPA Verification
* **Issuing Authority**: Educational Institution, University, Board of Education

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Student Name**: Legal name of the student.
* **Enrollment Number / Roll Number**: Student identifier.
* **Course / Degree Name**: Name of course/degree completed or in progress (e.g., "Bachelor of Science in Computer Science").
* **List of Subjects**: Names of courses, subject codes, and semesters.
* **Grades / Marks**: Grades (A, B, C, etc.) or percentage marks obtained in each course.
* **Cumulative GPA (CGPA) / GPA**: Overall grade point average or aggregate percentage.
* **Year of Passing**: Month and year of graduation/completion.
