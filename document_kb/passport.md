# Passport

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Passport
* **Alternative Names**: International Passport, Indian Passport, Travel Document
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Identity (PoI), Proof of Address (PoA), Proof of Date of Birth (PoB), Nationality Proof
* **Issuing Authority**: Ministry of External Affairs, Passport Seva (Government of India / National Governments)

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Passport Number**: Alphanumeric unique passport number.
* **Given Name**: First name and middle name.
* **Surname**: Last name / Family name.
* **Nationality**: Country of citizenship (e.g., "Indian", "American").
* **Date of Birth**: Date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY).
* **Place of Birth**: City and State/Country of birth.
* **Place of Issue**: Location where the passport was issued.
* **Date of Issue**: Date of issuance (DD/MM/YYYY).
* **Date of Expiry**: Date of expiration (DD/MM/YYYY).
* **Father's Name**: Father's legal name.
* **Mother's Name**: Mother's legal name.
* **Spouse's Name**: Spouse's legal name (if applicable).
* **Full Address**: Permanent address registered in the passport records.
