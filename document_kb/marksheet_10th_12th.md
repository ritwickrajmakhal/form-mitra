# 10th / 12th Standard Marksheet

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: 10th / 12th Marksheet
* **Alternative Names**: Matriculation Certificate, SSC Certificate, HSC Marksheet, Intermediate Certificate, High School Certificate
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Date of Birth (PoB), Proof of basic educational qualification, Grade verification
* **Issuing Authority**: State/Central Board of Education (e.g., CBSE, ICSE, State Boards)

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Candidate's Name**: Legal name of the candidate.
* **Father's Name / Mother's Name**: Parent's names.
* **Date of Birth**: Often printed on 10th marksheet (SSC) and considered a standard legal age proof in India.
* **Roll Number / Roll Code**: Registration numbers.
* **Subject Wise Marks / Grades**: List of subjects and corresponding score percentages or grades.
* **Result Pass/Fail**: Status of qualification.
