# Medical / Fitness Certificate

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Medical Certificate
* **Alternative Names**: Physical Fitness Certificate, Medical Report, Doctor's Certificate
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of physical fitness for admissions/employment, Proof of disability (if applicable)
* **Issuing Authority**: Registered Medical Practitioner (RMP), Civil Surgeon, Government Hospital Doctor

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Patient/Applicant Name**: Full name.
* **Age & Gender**: Personal demographic details.
* **Physical Stats**: Height, weight, chest expansion, vision/eyesight.
* **Fitness Declaration**: Certificate text saying "Fit for work/study/admission".
* **Doctor's Name & Registration Number**: The license details of the signing doctor.
* **Signature and Seal of Doctor**: Verification stamp.
