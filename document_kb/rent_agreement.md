# Rent Agreement / Lease Deed

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Rent Agreement
* **Alternative Names**: Lease Agreement, Tenancy Agreement, Lease Deed
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Temporary/Current Address (PoA)
* **Issuing Authority**: Notary Public, Sub-Registrar Office (Registered Rent Agreement)

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Tenant Name**: Name of the tenant (used as proof of residence).
* **Landlord Name**: Name of the property owner.
* **Property Address**: The rented premises address (used as Proof of Address).
* **Lease Term**: Start date, end date, and duration of tenancy (usually 11 months).
* **Rent Amount / Security Deposit**: Monthly rent and deposit details.
