# Work Experience / Relieving Letter

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Experience Letter
* **Alternative Names**: Relieving Letter, Service Certificate, Employment Reference Letter
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Work Experience, Verification of previous employment
* **Issuing Authority**: Previous Employer / HR Department

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Employee Name**: Name of the former employee.
* **Date of Joining (DOJ)**: Start date of employment.
* **Date of Relieving (DOR)**: End date of employment.
* **Last Designation / Job Title**: Last held role/position.
* **Duties/Responsibilities**: Brief list of job tasks (if detailed).
* **Company Letterhead / Seal**: Official authorization details.
