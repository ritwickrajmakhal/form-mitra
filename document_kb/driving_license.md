# Driving License

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Driving License
* **Alternative Names**: Driver's License, DL, DL Number
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Identity (PoI), Proof of Address (PoA), Driving Authorization, Age Proof
* **Issuing Authority**: Regional Transport Office (RTO), State Transport Department

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Driving License Number / DL Number**: Unique alphanumeric identifier (e.g., "DL No", "License Number").
* **Full Name**: Holder's name.
* **Father's / Husband's Name**: Relation name.
* **Date of Birth**: Date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY).
* **Full Address**: Holder's residential address.
* **Date of Issue**: Issuance date.
* **Validity Period**: Non-transport and transport validity dates (e.g., "Expiry Date", "Valid Till").
* **Authorized Vehicle Class**: Category of vehicles permitted to drive (e.g., MCWG, LMV).
* **Blood Group**: Holder's blood group (often displayed on newer licenses).
