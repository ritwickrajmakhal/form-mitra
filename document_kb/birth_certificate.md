# Birth Certificate

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Birth Certificate
* **Alternative Names**: Janam Praman Patra, Certificate of Birth
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Date of Birth (PoB), Proof of Parentage
* **Issuing Authority**: Municipal Corporation, Registrar of Births and Deaths, Local Panchayat

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Registration Number**: Unique registration identifier.
* **Child's Name**: Full name of the child (if named at registration).
* **Date of Birth**: Exact date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY and in words).
* **Place of Birth**: Hospital name or address where birth occurred.
* **Gender**: Sex of the child.
* **Father's Name**: Legal name of the father.
* **Mother's Name**: Legal name of the mother.
* **Permanent Address of Parents**: Address at the time of birth.
