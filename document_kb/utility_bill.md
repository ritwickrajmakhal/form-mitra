# Utility Bill (Electricity/Water/Gas)

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Utility Bill
* **Alternative Names**: Electricity Bill, Bijli Bill, Water Bill, Piped Gas Bill, Landline Telephone Bill
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Address (PoA) (usually must be recent, i.e., less than 3 months old)
* **Issuing Authority**: Utility Provider / Municipal Board (e.g., Power Distribution Company, Water Supply Board)

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Consumer Name / Customer Name**: Name of the owner or occupier.
* **Billing Address / Service Address**: The physical address where the utility is delivered (used as Proof of Address).
* **Consumer Number / Account Number**: Unique service ID.
* **Bill Date / Due Date**: Date the bill was generated (important for checking recency).
