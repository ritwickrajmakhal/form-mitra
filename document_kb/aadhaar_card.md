# Aadhaar Card (UIDAI)

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Aadhaar Card
* **Alternative Names**: Aadhaar, UID, UIDAI Card, Aadhaar Card Number
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Identity (PoI), Proof of Address (PoA), Proof of Date of Birth (PoB)
* **Issuing Authority**: Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI), Government of India

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Aadhaar Number**: A unique 12-digit random number issued to residents of India. (e.g., "Aadhaar Number", "UIDAI No", "Aadhaar No").
* **Full Name**: The resident's legal first name, middle name, and last name.
* **Date of Birth**: Format DD/MM/YYYY. (e.g., "Date of Birth", "DOB").
* **Gender**: Male, Female, or Transgender.
* **Father's Name / Care of (C/O)**: Usually listed in the address section (e.g., "C/O [Father's Name/Spouse's Name]").
* **Full Address**: Residential address including House Number, Street, Village/Town/City, District, State, and Pin Code (Postal Code).
* **Pin Code / Postal Code**: 6-digit postal code.
* **Linked Mobile Number**: Mobile number registered with UIDAI.
* **Linked Email ID**: Email registered with UIDAI.
