# PAN Card (Permanent Account Number)

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: PAN Card
* **Alternative Names**: Permanent Account Number, PAN, Income Tax PAN Card
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Identity (PoI), Financial Transactions, Tax Registration, Proof of Date of Birth (PoB)
* **Issuing Authority**: Income Tax Department, Government of India (via NSDL/UTIITSL)

## Fields and Information Contained
* **PAN Number**: A unique 10-character alphanumeric identifier. (e.g., "PAN", "PAN Card Number", "Permanent Account Number").
* **Full Name**: Legal name of the cardholder.
* **Father's Name**: Cardholder's father's name (mandatory field on PAN cards).
* **Date of Birth**: Format DD/MM/YYYY. (e.g., "Date of Birth", "DOB").
* **Signature**: Specimen signature of the cardholder.
* **Photograph**: Passport size photograph of the holder.
