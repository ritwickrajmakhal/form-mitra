# Income Certificate

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Income Certificate
* **Alternative Names**: Aay Praman Patra, Certificate of Family Income
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of annual household income, Fee concession eligibility, Scholarship claims, Subsidy claims
* **Issuing Authority**: Tehsildar, Revenue Department Officer, Sub-Divisional Magistrate (SDM)

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Applicant's Name**: Full name of the applicant or head of family.
* **Father's / Husband's Name**: Relation name.
* **Annual Income**: Total yearly earnings of the family/applicant in numerals and words.
* **Assessment Year**: The financial year for which the income is certified.
* **Residential Address**: Full address of the applicant.
