# Caste / Category Certificate

## Document Details
* **Document Name**: Caste Certificate
* **Alternative Names**: Jati Praman Patra, Community Certificate, Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
* **Primary Uses**: Proof of Caste/Reservation Category, Fee concession, Reserved seat applications
* **Issuing Authority**: District Magistrate, Tehsildar, Revenue Authority

## Fields and Information Contained
* **Applicant's Name**: Full name.
* **Caste / Sub-Caste Name**: The designated caste or category (e.g., Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe, OBC, EWS).
* **State / Union Territory**: State and district where the caste is recognized.
* **Father's Name**: Legal name of father.
* **Relevant Constitutional Order**: Citation of the legal order recognizing the caste.
